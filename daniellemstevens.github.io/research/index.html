Research

I am to combining genomics and molecular biology to understand the intersepction of effector biology and immune perception in Gram-positive plant-associated pathogens and their plant hosts. I also collaborate with other researchers to use my computational skillsets

If your interested in some of the work I do, please feel free to check it out.

Comparative Genomics of Clavibacter Bacteria

Clavibacter bacteria are host specific pathogens with many predicted virulence effectors. Clavibacter species cause disease on a narrow host range of plants, but their ability to colonize endophytically is broad.

Plant Immune Perception of Gram-positive Bacteria

Plant innate immune systems perceive and elicit responses to limit pathogens. Surface-localized pattern recognition receptors (PRRs) can perceive molecular features such as MAMPs, damage-associated molecular patterns, and effectors from diverse pathogens.
